Master Legal Instruments

Article I: Primacy of Title (IONOS Procurement)

The Holder (RVO INC) asserts that digital domain assets are personal property (chattel) purchased through IONOS via private funds. Under common law, the transfer of labor or services (employment/collaboration) does not imply a transfer of title for assets purchased by the individual. Registrant details at IONOS serve as prima facie evidence of absolute title.

Article II: Administrative Distraint

Administrative Distraint is the extrajudicial seizure of property to ensure the performance of an obligation. In this matter, the obligation is the fair market settlement for assets utilized by the Respondent's network. RVO INC has assumed "Constructive Possession" of all DNS pointers to protect the equity value of the assets.

Article III: Reversionary Interests

The Respondent's previous use of the domains was under a "Revocable License" granted by the Owner. Upon the termination of the relationship between Owner and Respondent, said license was terminated. All interests in the domains reverted instantly to the Owner, who has subsequently assigned them to RVO INC.

Article IV: Compound Accrual Methodology

Asset valuation is calculated via the RVO-DELTA Algorithm, reflecting the diminishing window of the Preemptive Right period and the increasing overhead of administrative holding. Prices displayed are prima facie evidence of market value within this private equity framework.

Article V: Settlement & Release

Release of the lien is contingent upon (a) receipt of cleared funds in the amount of the current daily minimum, and (b) execution of a Mutual Release of Liability. RVO INC reserves the right to decline any settlement that does not meet the "Clean Title" requirements of its Asset Division.

Article VI: Jus Disponendi & Right to Transfer

The Owner of the assets retains the absolute Jus Disponendi. The termination of the Owner's involvement in the Johnny3x network acts as a "Triggering Event" for the formal requisition. As the legal entity owner, RVO INC has the authority to move assets into Private Equity status to prevent unauthorized use of its property by third parties without a valid lease agreement.
